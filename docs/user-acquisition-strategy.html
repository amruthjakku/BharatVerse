üé® Creative Campaign Ideas

"Every Family Has a Story"

Emotional campaign highlighting the importance of preserving family heritage through user testimonials and multi-generational storytelling.

"Discover the India You Never Knew"

Focus on hidden cultural gems and lesser-known traditions with travel and exploration angle, partnering with tourism boards.

"Voices of Our Elders"

Campaign to encourage documenting elder's stories with intergenerational connection theme and emotional storytelling approach.

"Cultural Detectives"

Gamified approach to heritage discovery with mystery and exploration elements, focusing on community collaboration.

"Heritage Heroes"

Celebrate users making significant cultural contributions through recognition and awards program to inspire others.

"#MyHeritageStory Challenge"

Viral social media challenge encouraging users to share 60-second cultural stories with prizes and community recognition.
